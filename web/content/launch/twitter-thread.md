## Tweet 4 (Clustering)

4/ Each problem gets scored on:

- Frequency (how often mentioned)
- Severity (how painful)
- Market size (how many people affected)
- Technical complexity (how hard to build)
- Time to MVP (weekend → quarter)

Priority = Opportunity / Effort
