## Description

### The Problem
Indie hackers spend hours scrolling Reddit and Hacker News looking for problems to solve. By the time they find something interesting, they've forgotten it or someone else has already built it.

### The Solution
IdeaForge scrapes 300+ posts daily, clusters similar problems, scores them by opportunity vs effort, and delivers the top 10 to your inbox at 8 AM—with complete technical specs.

### How It Works

**1. Scrape**
We monitor Reddit (8 subreddits), Hacker News (Ask HN, Show HN, comments), Twitter (#buildinpublic, #indiehacker), and GitHub (500+ star repos).

**2. Analyze**
AI detects pain point signals like "I wish there was..." and clusters similar complaints together.

**3. Score**
Each problem gets scored on frequency, severity, market size, complexity, and time to MVP.

**4. Generate**
GPT-4 creates complete business briefs with technical specs, competitor analysis, and launch strategies.

**5. Deliver**
You wake up to an email with today's best ideas, ready to build.

### What's in a Brief?
- Problem statement and target audience
- Competitor analysis with market gaps
- Technical spec (stack, architecture, MVP scope)
- Business model and pricing strategy
- Go-to-market plan
- Risk assessment
