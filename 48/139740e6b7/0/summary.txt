Yes — Hetzner requires identity verification for new accounts. They typically ask for:

- **Government-issued ID** (passport or national ID card)
- **Credit card or PayPal** for billing

Some accounts get auto-verified with just a credit card, but many new signups get prompted to upload an ID photo. It's a one-time thing for fraud prevention.

If you'd prefer to skip that, **DigitalOcean** and **Vultr** usually only need a credit card — no ID upload. Similar specs would be around $48/mo (DO) or $24/mo (Vultr for 4 vCPU/8GB).
