Been working on something for a while and finally putting it out there. **ZeroToShip** scrapes Reddit, HN, and GitHub every morning for pain points people are complaining about, clusters them, and turns them into startup idea briefs using Claude. It lands in your inbox before you start your day. Built it because I was tired of guessing what to build and wanted to let real complaints guide the decision.
